How randomisation works in the real app

Each time a trip is first created, the app randomly assigns tile positions within a grid of possible anchor points, avoiding overlaps. The bezier control points are also randomised within defined ranges to keep the path curvy and interesting without ever looking rigid. The result is different every time.
