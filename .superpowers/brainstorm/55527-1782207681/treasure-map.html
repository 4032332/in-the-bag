In the real app, the background is an AI-generated Imagen 3 illustration themed to the destination. The tiles and dotted path are overlaid on top. Tapping a tile opens that day.

What you're seeing

  • Parchment-toned background with hand-drawn Japanese landmarks (Mt Fuji, Torii gate, temple, cherry blossoms)
  • Each day is a dark tile with a gold border, showing day number, date, and a summary of events
  • Dotted lines connect days in chronological order, snaking across the map
  • The backpack icon (bottom right) floats over the map to open "In the Bag"
  • Tapping a day tile drills into that day's events
  • A compass rose sits in the corner for authenticity

In the real app

  • Gemini Imagen 3 generates the background based on the destination (e.g. pirate map for Hawaii, samurai map for Japan)
  • The map style and colour palette adapt to the destination theme
  • Days can be expanded to show individual events within the map view
  • Premium feature only
